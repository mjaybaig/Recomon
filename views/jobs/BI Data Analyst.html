BI Data Analyst

Provide data solution for reporting needs involving data integration and blending. Identify and improve query performance. Translate business requirements of the key stakeholders into specifications that will be used to prepare combined data source. Be able to explain data through written, oral, and visual means to audiences with technical and non-technical background.

Skills Needed

  • MS SQL, Oracle,
  • Teradata or Hive
  • Communication skills, critical thinking and team work spirit
