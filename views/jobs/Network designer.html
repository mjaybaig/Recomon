Network Designer

Maintaining and administering computer networks and related computing environments including systems software, applications software, hardware, and configurations. Performing disaster recovery operations and data backups when required. Protecting data, software, and hardware by coordinating, planning and implementing network security measures. Troubleshooting, diagnosing and resolving hardware, software, and other network and system problems.

Skills Needed

  • knowledge on switches, Routers, Firewalls, LAN, WAN, TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, EIGRP, BGP, OSPF, NHRP, ATM, PPP, MPLS, VPN, Trunking, Packet capture
  • Adtran, Cisco, Brocade, Zhone, Palo Alto, Acme Packet, Sansay
  • Linux, Solaris, and Windows environments
  • Experience with at least one of the following vendors platforms: Adtran Voice Gateways, Cisco Call Manager, MetaSwitch, Genband, or Broadsoft
  • Troubleshooting LAN, WAN, VoIP, and various IP related concepts
